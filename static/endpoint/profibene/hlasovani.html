Les Collons, Švýcarsko

The Matterhorn Chalet is an expansive, five bedroom chalet which blends the best of old and new with traditional architecture and a bright and modern interior.

Designed by renowned local architect, Eric Papon, the chalet has been built to the highest standard with alpine wood and natural stone.

The chalet is in a sunny and open position at 1700m, is just a 2 minute drive from the shops and lifts and has stunning panoramic views to the Matterhorn.

It is very spacious and has the feel of a boutique hotel as well as offering a relaxing wellness area with a sauna and Jacuzzi.

The top floor features the main living space with high ceilings, a modern kitchen, dining area and sitting room.

Zadar, Chorvatsko

This property is a well-maintained apartment building in an exceptional location right on the seafront in Privlaka, Zadar region.

It is situated on a plot of approximately 756 m² and offers a living space of about 265 m² spread over three floors with the following layout:

Basement: Storage area with the possibility of conversion into an additional apartment or a wellness area.

Ground floor: Apartment with three bedrooms.

Upper floor: Two apartments - one with one bedroom and the other with two bedrooms.

The property and the associated outdoor area are in well-maintained condition. Fantastic location: just a few steps to the beach in front of the house.

Privlaka is a picturesque village on the Adriatic coast of Croatia, known for its beautiful sandy beaches and crystal-clear water. This charming village attracts many tourists, especially during the summer months, who are looking for relaxation and a natural setting.
